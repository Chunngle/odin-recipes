Jacket Potato Recipe

Description.

A recipe for a nice jacket potato. Read this if you want to make a tasty jacket potato.


For fans of potatoes.


Ingredients.

  • Potato
  • Cheese
  • Beans

Steps

  1. Grab a potato
  2. Cook said potato
  3. Add some grated cheese
  4. Get some beans
  5. Cook beans in a microwave
  6. Pour beans over Potato
  7. Thats it
