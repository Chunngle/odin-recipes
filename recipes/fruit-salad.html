Fruit Salad Recipe

Description.

A recipe for a Fruit Salad.


Follow along and you can make a fruit salad like this.


Ingredients.

  • Strawberries
  • Bannana
  • Blueberries
  • Apples

Steps

  1. Put strawberries in a bowl
  2. Chop up a bannana and add it to the bowl
  3. Get some blueberries and put them in the bowl
  4. Slice some apples and add them to the bowl
  5. Eat
